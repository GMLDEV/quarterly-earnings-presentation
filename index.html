Risk Assessment Algorithm

Our proprietary risk scoring system:


def calculate_risk_score(portfolio):
    """
    Calculate comprehensive risk score for portfolio
    """
    # Market risk component
    market_risk = portfolio.beta * market_volatility
    
    # Credit risk assessment
    credit_risk = sum([
        loan.probability_default * loan.exposure_at_default 
        for loan in portfolio.credit_exposures
    ])
    
    # Liquidity risk factor
    liquidity_risk = portfolio.illiquid_assets / portfolio.total_assets
    
    # Combined risk score (0-100 scale)
    risk_score = (
        0.4 * market_risk + 
        0.4 * credit_risk + 
        0.2 * liquidity_risk
    ) * 100
    
    return min(risk_score, 100)  # Cap at 100

Current Portfolio Risk Score: 23.5 (Low-Medium Risk)

This algorithm integrates multiple risk factors into a single score. Market risk uses our portfolio beta against market volatility. Credit risk aggregates individual loan default probabilities. Our current score of 23.5 indicates well-managed risk exposure.
